Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly membership with 30 days notice. Annual memberships have a 60-day cancellation policy.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The prices shown include everything. There's only a one-time $25 enrollment fee for new members.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel.

Do you offer corporate discounts?

Yes, we offer special corporate rates for companies with 10+ employees. Contact us for details.

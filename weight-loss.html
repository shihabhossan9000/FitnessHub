  • Burpees - 30 sec on, 30 sec rest x 8
  • Mountain Climbers - 30 sec on, 30 sec rest x 8
  • Jump Squats - 30 sec on, 30 sec rest x 8

  • Push-ups - 3 sets x 12 reps

Weight Loss Nutrition Tips

Calorie Deficit

Create a moderate calorie deficit of 300-500 calories per day for sustainable weight loss.

Whole Foods

Focus on nutrient-dense whole foods like vegetables, lean proteins, and complex carbohydrates.

Meal Timing

Eat regular meals and avoid late-night snacking to optimize your metabolism.

Hydration

Drink water before meals and stay hydrated throughout the day to support fat loss.
